Fast, simple, and built for your workouts.

Designed for the moment you’re lifting

Beam is made to be effortless during a workout. Each screen and button is tuned for speed and clarity so you can log sets quickly and stay focused.

Organized and private

Beam keeps your workouts organized — exercises, sets, reps, and weights — so your history stays consistent and easy to review. Everything is stored locally on your device and can be exported in open formats. Your data is yours.

Privacy built in

No accounts. No ads. No tracking. If you choose to share anonymous usage data, it helps improve the app. Nothing personal ever leaves your phone.
